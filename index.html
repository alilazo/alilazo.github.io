Tips & Tricks

A few things I've learned that supercharge my workflow.

01

AI Pair Programming

Treat AI tools like an intern. Give precise, bounded instructions and always review the generated code architecture before integrating it into your main branch. It speeds up boilerplate by 3x.

02

Minimalist Architecture

Adopt a "Scandinavian" approach to your codebase. Keep interfaces clean, functions pure, and avoid over-engineering. Negative space in your code (comments and readability) is just as important as in design.

03

Swiss Grid Layouts

When in doubt about UI, rely on rigid geometric grids and stark typography. High contrast not only creates an immediate 'premium' feel but completely solves accessibility contrast issues.
